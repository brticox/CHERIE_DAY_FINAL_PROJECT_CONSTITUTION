## Final Source Of Truth Notes

- Turkish-first editorial content uses `/rehber`, not `/journal`.
- All public customer-facing interface copy is Turkish.
- Commerce is Turkey-only for MVP: Turkish account, cart, checkout, payment, delivery, order tracking, proof approval, and support flows.
- Payments must support Turkish domestic cards plus foreign-issued Visa/Mastercard/AMEX where enabled by iyzico/PayTR merchant settings, with TROY support for Turkey.
- International commerce/shipping, multi-currency storefronts, EN/AR storefronts, live RSVP, and wedding website builder are future phases unless explicitly re-scoped.
- Current constitution readiness is **95/100** after the hardening lock files `22` through `31`.
- Assets must be referenced from this repository, not from `Downloads`, temporary clipboard folders, or desktop-only paths.
- External skill/plugin repositories are not part of this final constitution.

## Hero Rule

The generated wedding garden video is **background ambience only**.

The real interactive hero must be built from DOM-based transparent product layers controlled by:

- GSAP ScrollTrigger,
- Lenis smooth scroll,
- real HTML/CSS Turkish text,
- responsive layer positioning,
- reduced-motion fallback.

Brand marks, QR code, wax seal, wordmark, and ribbon branding must never be distorted, morphed, blurred, redrawn, translated, or regenerated by AI.

## Assets

Hero and brand source files are in `/assets`:

- `/assets/hero-source` — background video and visual references for hero layers.
- `/assets/brand-source` — SVG logo/stamp/monogram source files.

These are source/reference assets. Final implementation should optimize/export them into production-ready WebP/AVIF/SVG/MP4/WebM as needed.
