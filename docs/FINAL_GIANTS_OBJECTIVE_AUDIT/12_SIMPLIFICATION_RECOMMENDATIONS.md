# Simplification Recommendations

## Simplify MVP Without Losing Luxury

1. Launch with fewer product categories but better product pages.
2. Make most customized products quote-required at first.
3. Enable checkout only for standardized products with clear production/return terms.
4. Use a reduced mobile hero instead of reproducing desktop complexity.
5. Build one strong Rehber hub with 8-12 excellent articles, not dozens of thin pages.
6. Build minimal admin screens for content, leads, products, orders, proofs, and support only.
7. Defer advanced CRM automation.
8. Defer digital wedding website builder; market digital offerings as consultation/service first.
9. Defer full client portal; use account/order/proof pages first.
10. Use one or two hero motion moments, not continuous spectacle.

## Recommended MVP Shape

- Brand homepage with cinematic but performant hero.
- Core pages: Deneyimler, Koleksiyonlar, Mağaza, Dijital, Hatıra, Planlama, Rehber, İletişim.
- Product House with 10-20 strong products.
- Inquiry forms and WhatsApp fallback.
- Account/cart/order foundation.
- Checkout only for eligible products.
- Admin enough to operate, not enough to become a large back-office platform.
