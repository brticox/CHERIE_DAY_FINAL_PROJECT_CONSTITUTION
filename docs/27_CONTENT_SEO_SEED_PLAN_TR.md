## Turkish Microcopy Rules

- Prefer clear verbs.
- Avoid corporate stiffness.
- Avoid excessive poetry in forms, checkout, legal, and errors.
- Use warmth in confirmations and empty states.
- Do not use discount language.
- Do not use marketplace labels.
