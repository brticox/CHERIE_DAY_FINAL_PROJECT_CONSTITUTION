# External Inspiration Handling Policy

External sites and references may be used for taste, quality bar, interaction discipline, and ecommerce clarity.

They must not become fixed design instructions.

## Allowed Use

- identify what feels premium,
- study navigation clarity,
- study product storytelling,
- study checkout confidence,
- study motion restraint,
- study responsive behavior,
- study spacing and hierarchy.

## Forbidden Use

- copying layouts directly,
- copying hero composition directly,
- copying animations directly,
- copying colors without brand fit,
- forcing CHERIE DAY into another brand's design school,
- treating any reference as a required template.

## CHERIE DAY Rule

CHERIE DAY must be designed from:

- its own brand identity,
- Turkish luxury language,
- its commerce model,
- its product categories,
- its operational needs,
- the approved creative concept created for this project.

External inspiration is research only.
