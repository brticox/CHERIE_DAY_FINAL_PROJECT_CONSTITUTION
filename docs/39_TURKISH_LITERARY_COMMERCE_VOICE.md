# Turkish Literary Commerce Voice

This file defines the CHERIE DAY public copywriting voice. It governs all Turkish public-facing copy site-wide — not only the homepage — and works together with `12_CONTENT_STRATEGY.md` and `25_DESIGN_TOKENS_AND_TYPE_LOCK.md`. Where this file and `12_CONTENT_STRATEGY.md` overlap, this file provides the deeper literary discipline; `25_DESIGN_TOKENS_AND_TYPE_LOCK.md`'s exact microcopy tokens (`Seçimlerim`, `Güvenli Ödeme`, etc.) remain authoritative and must not be renamed.

Governing rule reminder: all public customer-facing UI and website copy must be Turkish (`00_AI_OPEN_FIRST_DESIGN_AND_BUILD_ORDER.md` §3).

---

## 1. Voice Principles

- **Breath-length lines.** Every poetic line must read comfortably in a single breath aloud — never longer than that.
- **Concrete imagery, not abstract praise.** Words like "muhteşem," "eşsiz," "unutulmaz" are banned as filler; the dove, the paper, the light, the velvet — the real objects of the Güvercin Bahçesi world — carry the emotion instead.
- **One sentence, one feeling.** Never stack two metaphors in a single sentence.
- **Respectful warmth.** "Siz" address throughout — the tone of a gracious host, never a salesperson.
- **Poetry opens, clarity closes.** Every section pairs one emotional line with one clear commercial line — they are never merged into a single overloaded sentence, and never reversed in order.
- **Musicality without forced rhyme.** Natural assonance/consonance is welcome; contrived end-rhyme is not.
- **Modern literary Turkish, not costume-Ottoman.** The register sits closer to modern Turkish literary prose (warm, direct, image-driven) than to heavy Divan-era vocabulary — luxury through precision, not archaism.

## 2. Forbidden Language Patterns

- Industry clichés: "hayalinizdeki güne birlikte adım atalım," "en özel gününüz," "masal gibi bir düğün"
- Exclamation-driven sales tone: "Hemen keşfedin!", "Kaçırmayın!"
- Fake urgency: "Sınırlı süre," "Son fırsat"
- Unnecessary English loanwords: "trend," "deal," "off," "must-have"
- Forced/cheesy rhyme
- Childish diminutives: "küçük dostlar," "minicik anılar"
- Robotic corporate phrasing: "Ürünlerimizi inceleyebilirsiniz," "Sitemizi ziyaret ettiğiniz için teşekkür ederiz"
- More than one metaphor per sentence
- Fake/loud discount language (already prohibited commercially by `09_COMMERCE_BIBLE.md`; also prohibited stylistically here)

## 3. Hero Headline / Subcopy Alternatives

| # | Headline | Subcopy | Note |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | "Bir davetiye, bir bahçe, bir ömür — CHERIE DAY'de başlar." | "200'den fazla davetiye, dijital davetler, nişan hediyeleri, yüzükler ve organizasyon — özenle sizin için." | Strongest commercial clarity |
| 2 | "Aşkınız burada yaprak açar." | "Davetiyeden kutlamaya, CHERIE DAY her anınızı ustalıkla tasarlar." | Shorter, more poetic |
| 3 | "Her mektup bir bahçeden gelir, her bahçe CHERIE DAY'den." | "Davetiye, dijital davet, nişan & söz, organizasyon — hepsi tek çatı altında, özenle." | Strongest storytelling voice |
| 4 | "Sevginiz, burada çiçek açar." | "Davetiyeden düğün organizasyonuna, CHERIE DAY'in imzasıyla." | Shortest, punchiest |

**Recommended: #1.** It keeps the poetic opening while naming scale ("200'den fazla"), format (dijital), and category breadth (nişan, yüzük, organizasyon) inside the first 12 seconds — satisfying both the emotional and commercial-clarity requirements simultaneously.

## 7. Three Tone Levels

| Level | Definition | Example |
|---|---|---|
| **Şiirsel Lüks (Poetic Luxury)** | Full emotional line, one metaphor layer | "Zarfın içinde, bir bahçe saklı." |
| **Dengeli Ticaret (Balanced Commerce)** | Poetic opener + clear commercial closer, two sentences | "Sözünüz, kadife kadar nazik olsun. Nişan tepsisi, söz hediyelikleri — imzamızla." |
| **Doğrudan Pratik (Direct Practical)** | Zero metaphor, full clarity | "Güvenli Ödeme · Özenli Teslimat · Tasarım Onayıyla Üretim" |

## 8. Tone Recommendation Map

| Area | Tone |
|---|---|
| Hero | Poetic Luxury |
| Atlas Gateways, 4 primary gateways, Hediyelikler, Yüzükler, Galeri | Balanced Commerce |
| Footer | Balanced Commerce (poetic close + direct nav/CTA) |
| Cart drawer | Balanced (only the empty-state is poetic; product rows are always fully direct) |
| WhatsApp/support CTA | Balanced / lightly poetic |
| Header, navigation menu, search results | Direct Practical |
| Checkout, account, forms, error states, order statuses | Direct Practical, no exceptions |
| Cookie consent | Direct Practical + one line of warmth |
| Trust/Payment/Legal strip | Direct Practical |

## 9. Governing Rule

This voice applies to every public-facing surface described across the constitution — homepage, category pages, product pages, experience pages, checkout, account, and admin-facing customer communications (order status emails, proof approval messages) — not only the opening cinematic experience. Any new public copy added to the site must be checked against Section 1 (principles) and Section 2 (forbidden patterns) before publishing.
