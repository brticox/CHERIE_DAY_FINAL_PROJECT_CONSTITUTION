# CHERIE DAY Mega Store & Service Atlas

This file expands the constitution so no builder misunderstands CHERIE DAY as a small invitation shop.

CHERIE DAY is a large Turkey-only celebration commerce and service maison.
It includes physical products, printed products, digital products, custom products, service packages, event organization, city-based delivery/service flows, inquiry flows, galleries, reviews, and admin operations.

## 1. Core Business Truth

CHERIE DAY must be designed as a large store plus service showroom.

It is not only:

- invitations,
- a digital invitation tool,
- a wedding landing page,
- a boutique gift shop.

It is a full celebration maison for:

- weddings,
- engagements,
- söz,
- isteme,
- birthdays,
- baby showers,
- gender reveal / baby celebration concepts,
- home celebrations,
- venue celebrations,
- corporate/private celebrations where approved,
- digital invitations and memory experiences,
- printed products,
- gifts,
- accessories,
- rings,
- organization services.

## 2. Store Architecture Principle

The store must handle hundreds of products and many service categories without feeling like a marketplace.

The structure should feel like:

- curated departments,
- collection worlds,
- event-type journeys,
- service showrooms,
- product catalog,
- customization studio,
- inquiry and reservation system.

It must never feel like:

- Trendyol,
- Amazon,
- vendor directory,
- random catalog,
- stock template shop.

## 3. Main Departments

### A. Davetiye & Basılı Ürünler

Physical printed invitation commerce.

Includes:

- physical invitation cards,
- envelopes,
- paper types,
- print finishes,
- foil / emboss / letterpress-like options where offered,
- quantity-based pricing,
- invitation suites,
- RSVP cards,
- menu cards,
- table cards,
- seating cards,
- thank-you cards,
- event stationery,
- printed bundles.

Scale expectation:

- The system must support around 200+ invitation designs and more later.
- Designs must be filterable by event type, style, color, material, price, production time, and customization level.
- Many products require proof approval before production.

Typical actions:

- `İncele`
- `Kişiselleştir`
- `Tasarımı Başlat`
- `Sepete Ekle` where fixed-price and checkout-ready
- `Teklif Al` where bespoke

### B. Dijital Davetiye & Dijital Deneyimler

Digital invitation commerce.

Includes:

- digital invitation templates,
- mobile invitation pages,
- website-style invitations,
- animated invitations,
- QR cards,
- digital memory links,
- digital album/memory pages where offered,
- design variations ready for customization and purchase.

Scale expectation:

- The system must support many digital designs, not only one custom service.
- Digital products may be checkout-enabled or proof-required depending on complexity.

Typical actions:

- `Önizle`
- `Kişiselleştir`
- `Dijital Davetiye Oluştur`
- `Sepete Ekle`
- `Tasarımı Başlat`

### C. Hediyelikler & Ziyaretçi Hediyeleri

Gift and favor commerce.

Includes:

- nikah şekeri,
- söz/nişan hediyelikleri,
- guest favors,
- gift boxes,
- keepsake boxes,
- personalized gifts,
- visitor gifts,
- premium packaging,
- ribbons,
- tags,
- small accessories.

Scale expectation:

- Dozens to hundreds of SKUs.
- Fixed-price sets and quote-based bespoke sets must both be supported.

Typical actions:

- `Sepete Ekle`
- `Koleksiyonunu Tamamla`
- `Kişiselleştir`
- `Teklif Al`

### D. Nişan, Söz & İsteme Ürünleri

Engagement and family ceremony product department.

Includes:

- nişan tepsisi,
- söz tepsisi,
- isteme presentation items,
- ring trays,
- cup/service details where offered,
- decorative sets,
- söz/nişan gift sets,
- family ceremony accessories,
- themed engagement boxes.

Typical actions:

- `İncele`
- `Kişiselleştir`
- `Sepete Ekle`
- `Teklif Al`

### E. Yüzükler & Aksesuarlar

Jewelry/accessory department where offered.

Includes:

- nişan yüzükleri,
- wedding/engagement ring concepts,
- ring boxes,
- presentation accessories,
- matching accessories.

Important:

- Ring products may require inquiry, sizing, material options, consultation, or quote.
- Do not force all jewelry into simple cart checkout unless pricing, stock, sizing, return, and legal rules are clear.

Typical actions:

- `İncele`
- `Ölçü / Seçenek Sor`
- `Teklif Al`
- `Randevu Al`

### F. Organizasyon & Konsept Hizmetleri

Service showroom and booking/inquiry department.

Includes:

- nişan organization,
- söz organization,
- isteme setup,
- wedding concepts,
- birthday organization,
- baby shower organization,
- gender reveal / baby celebration concepts,
- home setup,
- venue setup,
- table styling,
- decoration,
- flowers,
- candles,
- backdrop/photo corner,
- welcome table,
- stage/light setup where offered,
- DJ,
- live band / music service,
- photography/video service where offered,
- add-on services.

Service model:

- Some services are quote-required.
- Some may support reservation request.
- Some may be city-dependent.
- Some may require date, city, venue, guest count, event type, and concept selection.

Typical actions:

- `Konsepti İncele`
- `Teklif Al`
- `Rezervasyon Başlat`
- `Bize Danışın`
- `Şehrimde Hizmet Var mı?`

### G. Şehir Bazlı Hizmetler

Turkey-only city-based services.

The platform must support:

- city selection,
- service availability by city,
- event location details,
- home/venue option,
- travel/setup fee rules where needed,
- city-specific delivery/service notes,
- staff/admin assignment later.

Public UX:

- user can choose city,
- service pages can show availability or inquiry route,
- quote request captures city/date/venue.

### H. Galeri, Fotoğraflar, Değerlendirmeler

Trust and inspiration layer.

Includes:

- service galleries,
- product galleries,
- concept galleries,
- before/after where appropriate,
- customer reviews/testimonials,
- event stories,
- photo corners,
- featured real setups if approved.

Rules:

- Reviews must belong to CHERIE DAY experience, not third-party vendor ratings.
- Galleries must support service conversion and product discovery.
- Do not expose independent suppliers/vendors publicly.

## 4. Event-Type Navigation

The site must allow browsing by event type, not only by product category.

Event types:

- Düğün,
- Nişan,
- Söz,
- İsteme,
- Nikah,
- Kına where offered,
- Doğum Günü,
- Baby Shower,
- Gender Reveal / Bebek Kutlaması,
- Kurumsal / Özel Etkinlik where approved.

Each event type should connect:

- relevant products,
- relevant digital designs,
- relevant gifts,
- relevant organization services,
- relevant galleries,
- relevant quote/reservation flow.

## 5. Collection System

Collections are not only visual moods.
They are cross-department worlds.

A collection may include:

- printed invitation designs,
- digital invitation designs,
- gift/favor products,
- packaging,
- rings/accessories,
- engagement/wedding setup concept,
- service mood board,
- gallery examples,
- matching add-ons.

This lets the user shop a complete celebration world instead of isolated items.

## 6. Product/Service Behavior Types

The platform must support at least these behavior types:

- `cart_enabled` - fixed price, direct checkout.
- `proof_required_cart` - checkout allowed, production after design proof.
- `digital_checkout` - digital product with customization/delivery rules.
- `quote_required` - price depends on details.
- `reservation_request` - date/city/service inquiry.
- `inquiry_only` - high-touch service or premium item.
- `city_dependent_service` - service availability depends on city/date/location.

## 7. Required Filters

Product/service listing filters should support:

- subcategory,
- collection,
- color/palette,

- style,
- format: physical/digital/service/bundle,
- price range,
- production time,
- proof required,

- quote required,
- city availability for services,
- personalization available.

## 8. Admin Expansion Requirements

Admin must support the large store/service model.

Additional admin modules/fields:

- department management,
- event type management,
- subcategory management,
- service category management,
- city availability,
- service package builder,
- concept/gallery management,
- review/testimonial management,
- quote/reservation pipeline,
- product bulk import/export,
- invitation design library,
- digital design library,
- design/proof workflow,
- product/service relation builder,
- collection cross-sell builder,
- add-on builder,
- city/date/venue fields for service inquiries.

## 9. Homepage Requirement

The homepage must communicate within 8-12 seconds that CHERIE DAY is a large celebration maison with both:

- shoppable products,
- customizable/digital products,
- quote/reservation-based services.

The creative opening may be magical and cinematic, but it must reveal the breadth of the store quickly.

Suggested public clarity labels:

- `Davetiye`
- `Dijital Davetiye`
- `Nişan & Söz`
- `Hediyelikler`
- `Yüzükler`
- `Organizasyon`
- `Doğum Günü`
- `Baby Shower`
- `Müzik & DJ`
- `Şehir Hizmetleri`

## 10. Builder Warning

If an implementation agent builds only:

- a beautiful hero,
- a small invitation catalog,
- a simple gift shop,
- a few static product cards,
- an inquiry-only service page,

then it has misunderstood the project.

CHERIE DAY must be prepared as a large, scalable, Turkey-only celebration commerce and service platform.
