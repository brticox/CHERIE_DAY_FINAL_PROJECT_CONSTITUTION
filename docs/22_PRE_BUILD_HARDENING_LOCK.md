## MVP Lock

MVP includes:

- Turkish-first public Maison website,
- cinematic but performance-controlled homepage hero,
- experiences,
- collections,
- product house,
- inquiry-led shop,
- selective cart and Turkey-only checkout for eligible products,
- customer account,
- order/proof/support foundation,
- CMS basics,
- CRM lead basics,
- admin order/proof/support basics,
- Rehber SEO hub,
- Turkey legal/payment consent scaffolding.

MVP excludes:

- public marketplace behavior,
- public suppliers/vendors,
- international shipping/storefront,
- multi-currency storefront,
- EN/AR storefronts,
- live RSVP,
- guest-list tooling,
- self-serve wedding website builder,
- supplier portal,
- advanced analytics/automation.

## Day-One Commerce Decision

CHERIE DAY launches with **selective checkout**:

- standardized products can be cart-enabled,
- customized products can be quote-required or proof-before-production,
- bespoke/event work is inquiry-first,
- checkout is Turkey-only,
- payment supports Turkish domestic cards, TROY, and foreign-issued Visa/Mastercard/AMEX where iyzico/PayTR merchant settings allow,
- foreign-issued card acceptance does not imply international commerce.
