# SEO Bible

CHERIE DAY SEO should capture Turkish wedding and celebration intent while preserving the Brand House model. SEO should not create public vendor-marketplace pages.

## Clean URL Structure

Use short, Turkish-friendly, stable slugs:
- `/experiences/dugun`
- `/experiences/nisan-soz`
- `/shop/davetiye`
- `/collections/cherry-seal`
- `/digital/dugun-web-sitesi` or `/digital/wedding-website`
- `/rehber/evde-soz-fikirleri`

Avoid:
- city/provider directory URLs,
- comparison pages,
- public provider profiles,
- thin location pages.

## Metadata Patterns

Homepage:
- Title: `CHERIE DAY | Wedding & Celebration Experience House`
- Description: `CHERIE DAY düğün, nişan, kına, davetiye, dijital davetiye, ürün, film ve planlama deneyimlerini tek bir estetik dilde tasarlar, üretir ve koordine eder.`

Experience page:
- Title: `Düğün Deneyimi Tasarımı | CHERIE DAY`
- Description: `CHERIE DAY düğününüzü konseptten davetiyeye, dijital deneyimden gün koordinasyonuna kadar tek bir estetik dilde tasarlar ve sunar.`

Product page:
- Title: `[Product Name] | [Collection] | CHERIE DAY Product House`

Rehber article:
- Title: `[Search Intent] | CHERIE DAY Rehber`
- Description: Direct answer plus CHERIE DAY route.

## Schema Types

Use:
- `Organization`
- `LocalBusiness` or `ProfessionalService`
- `WebSite`
- `BreadcrumbList`
- `FAQPage`
- `Article`
- `Product`
- `CollectionPage`
- `Service`
- `ImageObject`
- `VideoObject` for Memory pages

Avoid schema that implies individual public providers.

## Internal Linking

Each page should link to:
- parent module,
- related collection,
- related products,
- relevant Rehber guide,
- quote request,
- portfolio/story proof.

Rehber articles should link into commercial pages naturally:
- Evde söz guide -> Nişan & Söz, Nişan Tepsisi, Hediyelik
- Davetiye guide -> Davetiye, Dijital Davetiye, Collections
- Düğün web sitesi guide -> CHERIE DAY Digital
- Budget guide -> Hayalini Tasarla / Quote Request

## Local SEO For Turkey

Use local SEO through brand/service pages and content, not mass city directories.

Recommended pages/content:
- Istanbul-focused examples if CHERIE DAY operates there.
- Turkey wedding planning guide.
- Destination weddings in Turkey guide.
- Istanbul engagement/söz ideas.
- Kına gecesi preparation in Turkey.

Avoid hundreds of thin city pages.

## Product SEO

Product pages should include:
- product name,
- category,
- collection,
- materials,
- personalization,
- event use cases,
- production time,
- matching items,
- image alt text,
- FAQ where useful.

## Service / Experience SEO

Experience pages should include:
- H1 with event type,
- process,
- inclusions,
- location/service area,
- portfolio examples,
- FAQ,
- related products/digital/memory.

## Collection SEO

Collection pages should include:
- collection story,
- mood/palette,
- included product types,
- matching digital theme,
- event styling direction,
- internal links to products and experiences.

## Blog / Rehber SEO

Core hubs:
- Evde söz fikirleri
- Nişan masası trendleri
- Davetiye modelleri
- Düğün web sitesi rehberi
- Hediyelik fikirleri
- Kına gecesi hazırlığı
- Düğün bütçesi
- Türkiye’de düğün planlama rehberi

Article pattern:
- search intent in H1,
- answer in first paragraph,
- descriptive H2s,
- FAQ,
- internal links,
- imagery,
- schema.

## Sitemap Strategy

Sitemaps:
- `sitemap.xml` index
- `sitemap-pages.xml`
- `sitemap-experiences.xml`
- `sitemap-products.xml`
- `sitemap-collections.xml`
- `sitemap-rehber.xml`
- `sitemap-media.xml` if needed

Exclude:
- admin,
- internal supplier/team pages,
- client portal private pages,
- quote form steps,
- duplicate filter states,
- thin search results.
